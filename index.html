A tidy pipeline

Our workflow blends Blender, Houdini (when needed), and Adobe/Substance 3D. We ship source files, well-named layers, and versioned exports so teams can integrate fast. Everything is tested in Unity and Unreal for scale, LODs, and draw-call friendliness.
