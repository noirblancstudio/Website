Crafted in Tokyo, guided by curiosity

Noir Blanc Studio is a small two-person design team based in Tokyo, working across 2D and 3D creation. We explore the harmony between precision and softness, from clean geometric compositions to expressive color textures. Every project begins with curiosity and evolves through play, blending artistic sensitivity with technical clarity.

A mindful design process

Our workflow focuses on thoughtful construction, from initial sketches to final assets. We use tools like Adobe CC, Blender, and custom scripts to build flexible, lightweight resources for visual storytelling, branding, and interactive experiences. Every texture, form, and pattern is tested to feel balanced, functional, and alive.
