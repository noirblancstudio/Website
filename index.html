Designed in Tokyo, built for play

Nori Blanc Studio creates patterns, graphics, and 3D digital assets for games and interactive brands. We favor clear forms, gentle colors, and delightful micro‑details that hold up in both print and realtime engines.

Point2

A tidy pipeline

Our workflow blends Blender, Houdini (when needed), and Adobe/Substance 3D. We ship source files, well‑named layers, and versioned exports so teams can integrate fast. Everything is tested in Unity and Unreal for scale, LODs, and draw‑call friendliness.
